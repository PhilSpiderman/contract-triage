# MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is made between **Ashford Partners LLP**, a limited liability partnership registered in England and Wales (OC399812) of 8 Cathedral Square, Lichfield WS13 7LD ("Ashford"), and the **Counterparty** ("Counterparty").

## 1. Purpose

The parties wish to discuss a potential professional services engagement (the "Purpose") and may share confidential information in connection therewith.

## 2. Definition

"Confidential Information" includes any non-public information disclosed by one party to the other, whether labelled confidential or not, including without limitation business plans, client lists, financial data, technical specifications, pricing models and personnel matters.

## 3. Restrictions on Use

The receiving party shall:

(a) treat all Confidential Information as strictly confidential;
(b) use it only for the Purpose;
(c) restrict access to its personnel and professional advisers on a need-to-know basis, all of whom shall be bound by confidentiality obligations.

## 4. Duration of Confidentiality

Each party's obligations of confidentiality under this Agreement shall remain in effect for **seven (7) years** from the date of disclosure.

## 5. Exclusions

The obligations of confidentiality do not apply to information that (a) is or becomes public through no fault of the receiving party; (b) was lawfully in the possession of the receiving party prior to disclosure; (c) is developed independently without reference to the disclosing party's information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law or regulatory authority (subject to prompt notice to the disclosing party where lawful).

## 6. No Assignment of Rights

This Agreement does not grant either party any licence or other right in the other party's intellectual property. Nothing in this Agreement obliges either party to enter into any further commercial arrangement.

## 7. Term

This Agreement enters into force on signature and continues for **twelve (12) months**, unless terminated earlier by either party on thirty (30) days' notice.

## 8. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of **England and Wales**, and the parties submit to the **exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts**.
