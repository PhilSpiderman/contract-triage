# MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **Riverside Consulting Limited**, a company incorporated in England and Wales (company number 09876543) with its registered office at 12 Mill Lane, Bath BA1 2AB ("Riverside"), and the **Counterparty** named below ("Counterparty"). Each may be referred to as a "party" and together as the "parties".

## 1. Purpose

The parties wish to explore a potential commercial relationship (the "Purpose") and may exchange Confidential Information for that purpose.

## 2. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by one party to the other, whether orally, in writing, electronically or otherwise, that is marked as confidential or would reasonably be understood by the recipient to be confidential.

Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the recipient; (b) was known to the recipient before disclosure; (c) is independently developed by the recipient without use of the disclosing party's Confidential Information; or (d) is rightfully obtained from a third party without restriction.

## 3. Obligations

Each party shall:

(a) hold the other party's Confidential Information in strict confidence;
(b) use such Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;
(c) not disclose such Confidential Information to any third party except to its employees, contractors and professional advisers on a need-to-know basis, and only where such persons are bound by confidentiality obligations no less protective than those in this Agreement.

## 4. Term of Confidentiality

The obligations under this Agreement shall continue for a period of **three (3) years** from the date of disclosure of the relevant Confidential Information.

## 5. Return or Destruction

Upon written request, each party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information of the other party in its possession, save for one copy retained for legal and compliance purposes.

## 6. No Licence

Nothing in this Agreement grants either party any rights in the other party's intellectual property, save for the limited right to use Confidential Information for the Purpose.

## 7. Term

This Agreement commences on the date last signed below and remains in force for **twelve (12) months**, unless terminated earlier by either party on thirty (30) days' written notice.

## 8. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of **England and Wales**. The parties submit to the **exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts**.
