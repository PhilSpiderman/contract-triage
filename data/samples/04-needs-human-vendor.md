# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into by and between **Halcyon Solutions Inc.**, a Delaware corporation having its principal place of business at 1209 Orange Street, Wilmington, Delaware 19801, USA ("Halcyon"), and the **Client** identified on the signature page hereto ("Client").

## 1. Services

Halcyon shall provide the consulting services described in one or more Statements of Work issued under this Agreement (the "Services"). All Services are provided on a non-exclusive basis except as set forth in Section 4 below.

## 2. Payment

Client shall pay all invoices issued by Halcyon within **ninety (90) days** of receipt. Any amounts not paid when due shall remain payable; no late payment interest or fees shall accrue except as expressly set forth herein.

## 3. Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of **three (3) years**, automatically renewing for successive one (1) year periods unless terminated for material breach pursuant to Section 7.

## 4. Exclusivity

During the term of this Agreement and for a period of **twenty-four (24) months thereafter**, Client shall not directly or indirectly engage any other consultant, vendor or service provider to provide services materially similar to those provided by Halcyon under this Agreement or any Statement of Work hereunder.

## 5. Limitation of Liability

Halcyon's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall be limited to the fees paid by Client in the six (6) months preceding the claim.

Client's liability under this Agreement shall be **unlimited** and shall include all direct, indirect, incidental, consequential, special and punitive damages arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, including without limitation damages for lost profits, lost data, business interruption and loss of goodwill.

## 6. Intellectual Property

Each party retains ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property. Halcyon hereby grants Client a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use the deliverables produced hereunder for Client's internal business purposes. All background IP, methodologies and tools used by Halcyon remain the property of Halcyon.

## 7. Termination

This Agreement may be terminated only by either party for material breach not cured within **sixty (60) days** of written notice specifying the breach in detail.

## 8. Confidentiality

Each party shall hold the other's confidential information in confidence for a period of three (3) years from the date of disclosure.

## 9. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement and any disputes arising hereunder shall be governed by the laws of the **State of Delaware**, USA, without regard to its conflict of laws principles. The parties hereby submit to the **exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in Wilmington, Delaware**.

## 10. Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with any Statements of Work, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations.
